# Ready2Spray AI - One-Page Business Plan (Updated with AI Credits)

## Executive Summary

**Ready2Spray AI** is an AI-powered agricultural operations management platform for aerial application companies and crop dusting services. The platform combines EPA compliance automation, intelligent job scheduling, and operational tracking with proprietary AI features that save operators 10+ hours per week on manual data entry.

---

## The Solution

1. **AI-Powered EPA Compliance** - Upload product labels, extract all EPA data automatically in 30 seconds (vs. 10 minutes manual entry)
2. **Intelligent Job Management** - Create, schedule, and track spray jobs with customizable workflows
3. **Map-Based Operations** - Draw field boundaries, upload flight paths, mark sensitive areas
4. **AI Copilot Assistant** - Ask questions, get instant answers about schedules, weather, and operations
5. **Enterprise API** - Connect with Zoho CRM, Salesforce, FieldPulse, Zapier, n8n, Make.com

---

## Target Audience

### Primary: Aerial Application Companies
- **Size:** 2-50 employees (pilots, dispatchers, office staff)
- **Revenue:** $500K-$10M annually
- **Pain Points:** Managing 100-1000+ spray jobs per season, tracking 50-200+ chemical products, coordinating 5-20 pilots

### Secondary: Pest Control Companies
- **Size:** 5-100 employees
- **Revenue:** $200K-$5M annually
- **Pain Points:** Recurring service plans, technician scheduling, chemical application records
